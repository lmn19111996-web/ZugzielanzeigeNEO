15-Day Energy Timeline
Horizontally scrollable t–E diagram, centered layout, task-colored segments, hover diagnostics, and a live “now” marker.
Range: Today + 14 future days
Input: Demo data, minute-scale feel, made-up values
Scroll with mouse wheel or ← → keys
Healthy band is emphasized in the center of the chart. Colored background strips represent task intervals. The energy line itself is recolored by the active task.
Interaction: hover the line, use mouse wheel to pan horizontally, or press the arrow keys while the chart is focused.
